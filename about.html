PM · Builder · Community Architect

I'm Ashley, a PM, builder, and community architect from Detroit. And, also a mom.

My path here wasn't traditional and that's exactly the point. I grew up homeschooled, went dual enrolled, and built my foundation outside the system before the system had a chance to define me. Before tech, I worked in healthcare where I learned what it actually means to design for people when the stakes are real.

I got into product by doing the work before I had the title. Writing PRDs. Leading teams. Building an audience of 100k+ before I had a brand strategy. I hold a Google PM certification and a Lean Six Sigma Green Belt, placed top 6 out of 90+ at TechTown's Mobility Week Hackathon, and shipped a SaaS product called PulsePoint.

I manage community at DevsCreate313, host events on cognitive accessibility, and founded a bootcamp on boundaries with a second one already in the works.
